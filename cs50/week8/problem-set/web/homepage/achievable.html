This element focuses on the importance of the goal to you and what you can do to make it attainable. You may need to develop new skills and changing attitudes after contemplating the viability of your goal. Don't forget, the goal is meant to inspire motivation, not discouragement. Ensure to think about:

  • How best to accomplish the goal.

  • Whether or not you currently have the skills needed to accomplish the goal

  • If not, what it would take to acquire them and if that is feasible for you.
